Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Rachel Miller, RHM Events & Weddings began as a small, family-run business with a big dream: to create extraordinary events that leave a lasting impression. Over the years, we've grown into a leading event planning company, known for our meticulous attention to detail, creative flair, and personalized service. Our journey has been filled with countless successful events, each one a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

At RHM Events & Weddings, our mission is to transform your dreams into reality. We believe that every event should be a reflection of your unique style and personality. Our team works closely with you to understand your vision, offering expert guidance and support every step of the way. We strive to exceed expectations, creating seamless and memorable experiences that you and your guests will cherish forever.
